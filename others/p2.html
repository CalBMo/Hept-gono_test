PARANOIA

"I've been losing my mind for five days. I didn't know who to talk to, I have no one. I decided to write this. I don't know if it will reach anyone. I recently got back from the psychiatrist I went to in the hope of finding adequate treatment, but it didn't seem to have had any effect. No matter where I am, they... well, I might even be alone, it doesn't matter. I always feel their gazes. I hear voices every second, they call me and sing and play this damn music and I don't... I don't know if I'm just hallucinating or if those things are really there, calling me. Those shadows. I can't stand them anymore. DAMN SHADOWS. I haven't slept for a few days but it's not like sleep deprivation would make things worse, this is not natural, I'M NOT GOING CRAZY. But I can't sleep. I've tried everything. Medicine, coarse salt... I've never believed in any of that, but I don't even know what to believe anymore. If I believe my own vision, what I hear... I will have to believe these demons I see too. I am scared. I need help. People call me crazy BUT I'M NOT. SOMEONE BELIEVE ME PLEASE!"

- Francis Summer - statment #0932317 taken in person
